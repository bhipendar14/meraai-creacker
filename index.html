👑 MeraAi Checkers

🎮 How to Play

  • Click a piece to select it, then click a valid square to move
  • Jump over opponent pieces to capture them
  • Chain multiple jumps in one turn if possible
  • Reach the opposite end to become a King (♔)
  • Kings can move both forward and backward
  • Capture all opponent pieces to win!
